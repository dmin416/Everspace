# Manga Outline

This outline follows the **manga** version of the story, which has better flow than the novel.

The prose chapters in `Story/` are being rewritten from the **novel** source. Both are rewritten to fit D's style and personality. Use this outline as the target structure and pacing guide when reorganizing or writing new material. Do not assume the prose chapters already match this outline beat for beat.
