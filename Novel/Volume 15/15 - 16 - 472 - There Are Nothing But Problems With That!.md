The next day, we were welcomed by Chris at the Earl Dareinwald House estate in the Imperial Capital, and we ended doing some light shopping... *Well, it wasn't really light shopping, was it? We spent almost half a day walking around, buying various household goods like the latest bedding. That isn't light at all, is it?*

「Hmm, this one feels softer.」

「It's nice that it's soft, but won't it be too hot to sleep with?」

「I'm sensitive to the cold, so something like this would be just right for me. Chris-chan, you're the same, right?」

「I used to be, but ever since I got body enhancements, I've become more sensitive to heat...」

Mimi, Elma, and Chris were enthusiastically discussing something that looked like a blanket. *I didn't realize that enhancing your body could change your constitution and make you more sensitive to heat...*

「Isn't it best if it's just easy to wash?」

「Choosing the right bedding is important, Sis. The quality of your bedding determines the quality of your life.」

「Ya are too serious about this, Wee...」

Tina was trying to prioritize cheap and easy-to-wash bedding, but Wiska, with a serious look on her face, stopped her, leaving Tina a bit taken aback... *Wiska sometimes has these weird fixations.*

「I'm more interested in this chair than the bedding.」

「I think it's better if you sleep in a proper bed, Doctor.」

「No, no, sleeping in a chair can be quite nice, you know? If the quality is good, it's no different from a bed.」

Just choosing bedding-and some non-bedding items- created a lot of chaos so we stood out a lot. People looked at us with faces that said, 'What a strange group.' Well, with Chris and her bodyguards included, we were a group of ten people. It wasn't an exaggeration to call us a large group. We attracted a lot of attention. To finish it, Chris brought bodyguards who were clearly identifiable at a glance. It was obvious that Chris was a lady of noble status. *Strictly speaking, she's not just a lady but the future Countess, though. Still, it seems that if she doesn't openly declare her status, she can just barely pass as the daughter of a high-ranking noble.*

*In reality, the governor of an entire star system is usually treated on par with a baron even if that person is a noble's daughter without official titles. This means that it is usually not permissible for such a person to directly pick out items in a store with friends. Generally, people like that summon merchants to their mansion to do the shopping, at least in the Imperial Capital.*

*What about me, an honorary viscount? Apparently, I can do the same if I wanted, but honorary titles have a somewhat ambiguous status. They are not inheritable and don't count as true nobility. However, when it comes to the hierarchy among nobles, things get complicated.* *Mei explained it to me in detail, but it went in one ear and out the other. I don't remember much. Haha.*

After finishing our shopping, we returned to the ship.

「I can't allow it, Milady.」

「It's fine. There's no place safer than here.」

「The Master has given strict orders, so I can't let this pass. Milady, let's return to the estate.」

「I refuse.」

We were having tea on the ship, and just as we were about to think about dinner, the dispute broke out.

「I'll stay on the Black Lotus tonight. Edeltrud, you can return to the estate.」

「So, you're saying, Milady, that you'll stay here alone?」

「Yes, is there a problem with that?」

「There are nothing but problems with that!」

The future Countess Dareinwald and her knight bodyguard started squabbling. *Well, for them it's probably a serious argument, but*... *It sure is peaceful.*

「Are you just going to watch?」

I was watching the situation unfold, with my uncarbonated pseudo-cola in hand, when Elma spoke to me. *Hmm, honestly,* *I don't care either way.*

「Should I intervene?」

「That's up to you, Hiro...」

Elma glanced towards Mimi, who was watching Chris and her bodyguard Edeltrud argue with a worried expression. *Sigh, I guess I have no choice.*

「Okay, that's enough. Let's stop arguing here. There's a girl about to cry.」

「Ah! My apologies Hiro-sama.」

Chris shrank back when I spoke to her.

「Hey, I'm not mad, so you don't have to shrink back so much. Edeltrud, you are concerned about me sleeping under the same roof as Chris, right? Ah, you don't need to respond; it's clear enough. Chris, you're not trying to use this as an opportunity to take things to the next level between us, are you? You just want to spend the night with Mimi and the others after a long time, right?」

「Y-yes, that's right... I think?」

Her response was a bit uncertain, but I got the confirmation I needed. *That'll do.*

「Alright, then here's what we'll do. I'll spend the night elsewhere tonight. If you're really worried about Chris, Edeltrud, you can stay on the Black Lotus as well. That should resolve everything, right?」

「N-no, no, no! That doesn't resolve anything! You understand what it means for a woman to spend the night on a man's ship, don't you?」

「It's the first time for you, Edeltrud so it might affect your reputation, but for Chris, this isn't anything new, is it? She's already spent a lot of time on my ship before and we even stayed several days on a resort planet together. Besides, if we're going to be officially engaged or married in the future, rumors of us having that sort of relationship could actually be beneficial, don't you think? As for the real thing, I don't plan to do it until I get direct approval from Earl Dareinwald himself.」

「Well, that's... maybe true?」

Edeltrud tilted her head. *This is just my opinion, though. I don't know what you think, and I lack the general knowledge of noble etiquette to judge properly.*

「Anyway, if your concern, Edeltrud, is me, then everything should be settled with this. The rest is up to you. I'll spend the night out with Mei.」

「...You're going with Mei?」

「Is that a problem? She stayed behind two days in a row to look after the ship and Neve, so I want to show her some appreciation.」

*Though Mei doesn't eat, drink, or really have any material desires, so there's not much I can do for her. If anything, letting her take care of me is probably the best reward I can give her.*

「...If that's the case, then there's no helping it.」

Elma agreed, though she seemed a bit disappointed. *Look, even I can understand that if I choose anyone other than Mei in this situation, it would cause friction. So, don't look so disappointed, please.*

*Also, Mimi and Chris, stop making those faces of regret like your plans have been foiled. Especially you, Mimi. It seems like Mimi has been trying to push Chris and me together rather forcefully. In that sense, I'm already checkmated, so she should just let things take their course without rushing. That's what I think, anyway.*

Now, having checked in and completed the room inspection, Mei was taking care of me while expressing how thankful she was.* It might sound a little racy, but she is only serving me some high-quality tea from the in-room tea set and expressing her gratitude.*

*Talking about future plans and possibilities with Mei is pretty fun. She's a great listener and takes even my most random thoughts seriously.*

I spent the night alone with Mei, chatting leisurely, and having her massage my tired body, it was thoroughly relaxing. *I can't help but wonder what kind of conversations the others are having back on the ship. On second thought, maybe it's better not to know. Yep, I better forget about it.*
