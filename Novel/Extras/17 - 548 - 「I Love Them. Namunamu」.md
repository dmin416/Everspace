After wrapping up our stay in the Holy Vuelzarus Empire, we were en route home. That said, it wasn’t a particularly long journey—just traveling from the core system of the Holy Vuelzarus Empire to the nearby star system where the gateway was located. From there, the Vuelzarus gateway could connect directly to the one in the Grakkan Empire’s capital, Grakkius.

Still, the path between the core system and the gateway-equipped system wasn’t exactly safe. The Holy Vuelzarus Empire had unstable dimensional boundaries, and space monsters frequently emerged from the other side. That’s why the Black Lotus had to move under heavy escort.

If we were on a Vuelzarus ship equipped with a psionic jump drive, we could’ve just skipped straight to the gateway system. But the Black Lotus didn’t have that kind of drive. Krishna apparently gained the ability after its evolution, but we haven’t tested it over long distances between star systems yet—only short jumps within visual range.

「Mornin’, Boss. Mornin’ to you too, Mimi, Elma-neesan.」

「Good morning.」

After the three of us took a leisurely shower and headed to the mess hall, we found Tina and Wiska already having breakfast.

「Morning. You’re both taking it slow today, huh?」

「We stayed up late organizing all the psionic tech we learned—takin’ notes, sortin’ it by field, that kind of thing.」

「Ended up oversleeping a bit.」

「Makes sense.」

The twin dwarven engineers, Tina and Wiska, had learned a lot during this trip to the Holy Vuelzarus Empire and were still in the process of making that knowledge their own. Apparently, they also managed to bring back a ton of technical documents and data. With any luck, they’ll surprise us with some cool results soon.

The three of us—Mimi, Elma, and I—collected our usual breakfast orders from the automatic cooker, Tetsujin V, and sat down to eat with the mechanic sisters. I had a typical Japanese-style set meal. Mimi got something like a sweet porridge. Elma had steak made from synthetic meat, plus a mountain of mashed-potato-like substance. I always wonder how she manages to wolf down that much meat and starch first thing in the morning. That must be the source of her gorilla-like strength.

Compared to when she first came aboard, Neve looks so much healthier now. Back then, she could barely survive without being hooked up to life support. Now she can walk around on her own. Hyperlane entry points—or rather, exit points—were usually located at the star system’s outer edge. Because placing a spacetime-warping gateway too close could interfere with the hyperlane itself, gateways were built far from the entry points, on the opposite edge of the system.

Depending on the star system, it could take several hours to cross from one outer edge to the other, even with FTL drives. On average, it was over ten hours. In the worst cases, nearly a full day. So yeah, we would still be in transit for a while even after arrival.

And if that doesn’t work, we’ll just run for it!
