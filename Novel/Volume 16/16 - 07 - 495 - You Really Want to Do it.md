Mimi, her face red, tried to cover her chest. *Oh, the bunny suit. The bunny suit. Oh, the bunny suit. Hmm, they are close to spilling out, but somehow they aren't. I'm not sure how that works, but it's probably due to some unnecessarily advanced technology.*

Dr. Shouko, wearing her usual attire minus the lab coat, was laughing as she watched everyone in their cosplay outfits. Neve, who was making a comment about Dr. Shouko, was dressed in a pure white one-piece dress. With her pale skin, white hair, and white dress, Neve looked like a vision in white. *If you only look at her appearance, she seems like a delicate, ephemeral girl, the dress suits her well, but knowing her true nature I can't really say it fits her.*

Despite her completely expressionless face, Mei exuded an intense sense of pride as she turned around and showed me the cat-like tail extending from her waist. *Oh, so it has a tail hole, just like Kugi's maid outfit. No, wait, that's not the point here.*

The cat ears standing straight up on Mei's head and the tail swaying back and forth behind her were clearly displaying how proud she was of the attachment. *It's funny how her lack of expression still manages to convey so much emotion.*

「You're seriously saying that? That's just... wow, that's a bit much, even for you...」

「I feel like I'm on top of the world.」

「I don't mind doing this sometimes...」

Tina defended me as she pressed herself against my body, draping herself over me. Wiska agreed with her modestly and also snuggled closely up to me.

「Let me just say it again: as a mercenary, you are really exceptionally well-behaved, Hiro. It's not uncommon for mercenaries to drown in booze, drugs, and women as soon as they have some money. You only have several women on your ship, and we are here by our own volition. Compared to those guys you are practically an angel, Hiro.」

Hearing about the typical behavior of less ethical, womanizing mercenaries from Elma, Chris's face turned red. Next to her, Mimi also looked a bit flustered, her cheeks slightly flushed, but her reaction wasn't as strong as Chris's. This was likely because Mimi had seen and heard plenty of such stories since she joined the crew. *She often talks alone with the reception ladies at the Mercenary Guild, it seems they are worried about her, *I've head them ask her questions like:  'Aren't you working for a captain who surrounds himself with lots of women? Are you alright? Are you being treated poorly?'* I find these insinuations quite offensive really. I treat my crew with the utmost gentlemanly respect. What?
