「...I'll do my best to adjust my perception.」

*I understand it in my head, but when I look at Neve's appearance... Her height isn't much different from Tina and Wiska, but her body is so thin. Really slender. Thin and delicate.*

「If you stare at me that much, even I'll get embarrassed... So, could you wait just a little longer? I'll ask the doctor to hurry and make me capable of handling such activities.」

「Aye aye, Captain. So, you did end up getting cozy, didn't you?」

「I told you we didn't! You really like these topics, don't you? Are you that interested, you pervy brat?」

I tapped on the glass of Neve's mobile pod again, but this time, her response was surprisingly serious, both in tone and expression.

「Of course I'm curious. Until now, I couldn't even move my body freely. I thought I'd just die without experiencing anything a normal human would. But now that I have a chance at a healthy body, of course I'm interested. Isn't that natural? Despite how I look, I'm a 24-year-old woman whose body is recovering its normal functions.」

「Well... yeah, that's true.」

「Plus, I've recently started being able to eat some flavored liquid foods. Sweetness is an amazing thing, Captain. Even when my stomach is full, I want to keep eating.」

*For some reason, listening to Neve makes me feel a bit sad. The sweet liquid food she is talking about is probably just a basic nutritional paste from a food cartridge with minimal processing and simple flavoring. It provides all the necessary nutrients, but it's far from what anyone would call delicious.*

「When your body is in better shape, and you can eat regular food, let's have some really good meals together, okay? Right, Mimi?」

「Yes! I'll prepare lots of delicious things that Neve-chan will love!」

Mimi, who had been quietly watching the exchange between Neve and me, clenched her fist with enthusiasm. *When Mimi gets excited like this, she tends to come up with dishes like 'alien creatures that look like they might attach themselves to your face and parasitize you (mild, creamy flavor)' I'm getting a bit worried... well, if it comes to that, I'll step in and help. I've gotten pretty used to the bizarre delicacies Mimi prepares. Surprisingly, they often taste pretty good... though there are some spectacular misses from time to time.*

「I've been waiting for you, Hiro-sama!」

「Oh, uh... sorry for keeping you waiting. I was a bit worn out and had to take a break.」

「You met with the people from Holz House, didn't you? In that case, it's understandable.」
