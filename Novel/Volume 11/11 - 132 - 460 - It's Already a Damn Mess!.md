Intruders would be mercilessly attacked by the Imperial Space Force's Imperial Guard Fleet and shot down, even if the intruding ship were affiliated with the Imperial Space Force.

That's why it was necessary to go through some complicated procedures first before one could pass through the capital's security. Well, it looks like Captain Serena would take care of said procedures herself just like last time, so we'll have to stay at the Grakkius Secundus colony until our planetary descent application gets approved.
